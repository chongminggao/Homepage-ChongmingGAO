I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), where I also completed my Ph.D. under the guidance of Prof. Xiangnan HE (何向南). My expertise lies in information retrieval and recommender systems. I hold both M.Eng. and B.Eng. degrees from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), supervised by Prof. Junming SHAO (邵俊明). I have also interned at leading tech companies such as Kuaishou, Alibaba DAMO Academy, and Alibaba Group’s AI Labs, focusing on conversational recommender systems and AI applications in social science.
